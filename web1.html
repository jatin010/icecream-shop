CHOCO ICECREAM
Combine 2 cups of heavy cream and 1 cup of whole milk in a bowl
In a separate bowl, whisk together 1 cup of sugar, 1/2 cup of cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt until well-mixed .
Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring until sugar dissolves.
Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions
Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until set.
Scoop and enjoy your homemade chocolate ice cream!
